Differences in the linguistic description

Most elements of the LMF vocabulary are defined by data values attached to the given instances, for example LMF says that a lexical entry may have properties such as partOfSpeech and grammaticalGender. In lemon such annotations are obtained by use of linguistic description ontologies, such as ISOcat.
